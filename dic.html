DEVELOPMENT AND INNOVATION CENTER

               

The Development and Innovation Center, often referred to as DIC, is a dynamic and forward-thinking technical club that serves as a hub for fostering creativity, innovation, and skill development within the world of technology. This club is dedicated to empowering its members to explore new horizons in the tech industry, solve real-world problems, and drive technological advancements.

VR AR MR

               

At the VR/AR/MR club, students are dedicated to pushing the envelope when it comes to bleeding edge technology.
